Directives for caching mechanisms in both requests and responses.

## Description
Directives such as `max-age`, `no-cache`, `no-store`, and `must-revalidate` give granular control over how long and where responses are cached.

## Usage Note
Headers are case-insensitive, although they are conventionally written in `Train-Case`.
